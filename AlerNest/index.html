The AlertNest Solution

AlertNest addresses the challenges of the emergency response system in Côte d'Ivoire with a user-friendly mobile application that leverages cutting-edge technology.

About

AlertNest provides faster emergency intervention and improved accessibility and inclusion. The application offers automatic geolocation to efficiently locate emergencies and real-time communication to eliminate delays and confusion. With a simple interface and multilingual support, including local languages, the app caters to a broader population. Additionally, AlertNest is designed to work with feature phones in areas with limited smartphone penetration.

Service

Emergency Reporting

An easy-to-use interface for rapid and accurate reporting of various emergencies (fire, medical, security) with automatic geolocation.

Real-Time Communication

Secure two-way chat with emergency dispatchers for clear communication and faster response.

AI-Powered Assistant (MVP Feature)

A chatbot providing first aid instructions, emotional support, and multilingual translation to overcome language barriers. This feature can be included in the minimum viable product or launched later.

Public Awareness Features

Educational content, safety tips, and access to emergency contact information for various situations.
